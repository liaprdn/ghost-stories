ABOUT

Ghost Stories, Coldplay 6th album will take you into a hauntingly introspective journey through themes of love, loss, and emotional resilience. Released in 2014, this album marks a departure from the band's signature anthemic sound, embracing a more subdued and atmospheric approach. A profound and immersive musical experience, leaving an indelible mark long after the final note fades away.
